Frequently Asked Questions

  • You can book an appointment through our contact form, by calling our office at +45 32 12 95 40, or by visiting our clinic directly. We offer flexible scheduling options Monday through Saturday.

  • We accept all major Danish health insurance providers including Danmark, Tryg, Alka, and Europeiske. We also work with the national health service and offer private payment options.

  • Yes, we have a 24/7 emergency hotline at +45 32 12 95 99. For life-threatening emergencies, please call 112. Our emergency department is staffed with experienced physicians round the clock.

  • Please bring your health insurance card, photo ID, list of current medications, and any relevant medical records or test results. Arriving 15 minutes early helps complete necessary paperwork.

  • Yes, we offer secure video consultations for follow-up appointments, prescription renewals, and non-emergency medical advice. Schedule a telemedicine appointment through our contact form.

  • We're open Monday to Friday from 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM, and Saturday from 9:00 AM to 2:00 PM. We're closed on Sundays and major Danish holidays. Emergency services are available 24/7.
